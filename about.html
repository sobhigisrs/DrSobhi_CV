Executive Biography

Profile

Dr. Sobhi Abdeljawad operates at the intersection of academic research, applied geomorphology, enterprise GIS, remote sensing, and institutional consulting. His career spans national environmental monitoring, disaster response mapping, utility infrastructure GIS, municipal GIS modernization, hydrological modeling, university teaching, and GeoAI research.

He has contributed to spatial decision-support systems for governmental agencies, environmental authorities, municipalities, utilities, universities, and development-oriented organizations. His work is distinguished by a practical ability to translate satellite data, field evidence, terrain models, and geodatabases into decisions that leaders can use.
